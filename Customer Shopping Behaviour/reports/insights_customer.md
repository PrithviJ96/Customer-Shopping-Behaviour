# **Customer Shopping Behavior Insights:**



1. Male customers generate approximately 2x higher revenue compared to female customers.

2. Products such as gloves, sandals, boots, hats, and handbags have the highest average ratings, indicating potential for premium pricing strategies.

3. Express shipping shows the highest average purchase value, suggesting an opportunity to prioritize and promote faster delivery options.

4. The number of non-subscribed customers is twice that of subscribed customers, despite having similar average spending, indicating potential for subscription conversion strategies.

5. Products like hats, coats, sneakers, sweaters, and pants exhibit the highest discount rates, showing strong dependence on discount-driven sales.

6. The loyal customer segment accounts for \~80% of the customer base, highlighting strong retention, followed by returning and new customers.

7. Young adults contribute the highest revenue, followed by middle-aged, adult, and senior groups, indicating key target demographics.
