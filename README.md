# 📊 Customer Shopping Behaviour Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Understanding customer shopping behavior is essential for improving sales strategies, customer retention, and overall business performance.
This project analyzes customer transaction data to uncover **key patterns, trends, and actionable insights** that can support data-driven decision-making.



## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing patterns and behavior
* Identify high-value customer segments
* Evaluate the impact of discounts, shipping types, and subscriptions
* Generate actionable insights to improve revenue and customer engagement



## 📂 Dataset

Source: Retail Customer Dataset
The dataset contains information on:

* Customer demographics (age, gender)
* Purchase details (amount, product category)
* Review ratings
* Subscription status
* Shipping type
* Discounts applied



## 🧠 Project Workflow

1. **Data Cleaning**

   * Handled missing values using category-wise mean imputation
   * Corrected data types and removed inconsistencies
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

   * Analyzed revenue distribution by gender and age group
   * Studied customer segmentation (loyal, returning, new)
   * Evaluated impact of discounts and shipping methods
3. **Data Analysis**

   * Identified top-performing product categories
   * Examined customer spending behavior
   * Analyzed subscription vs non-subscription trends



## 📈 Key Insights

* Male customers generate approximately **2x higher revenue** compared to female customers
* Products such as **gloves, sandals, boots, hats, and handbags** have the highest average ratings, indicating potential for **premium pricing**
* **Express shipping** is associated with higher average purchase value
* Non-subscribed customers are **twice as many** as subscribed customers despite similar spending, indicating an opportunity for **subscription conversion**
* Products like **hats, coats, sneakers, sweaters, and pants** rely heavily on **discount-driven sales**
* The **loyal customer segment (\~80%)** dominates the customer base
* **Young adults contribute the highest revenue**, making them a key target group



## 📊 Business Recommendations

* Promote **subscription plans** to increase customer lifetime value
* Optimize **discount strategies** for products heavily dependent on promotions
* Invest in **fast shipping options** to increase purchase value
* Target **young adult customers** with tailored marketing campaigns
